Our Story

The heart behind the harness

For years, we served the pet community as dedicated Veterinary Technicians. We saw too many wonderful dogs struggle in exams because they were overwhelmed, fearful, or reactive. That experience is what drove us to create Canem Arca. We bridge the gap between "wild animal" and "best friend," so your dog is a pleasure to live with and a breeze to care for.
