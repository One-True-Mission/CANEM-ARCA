Training built for the real world

Household Harmony

We specialize in transforming your bond through clear communication, professional expertise, and results-driven methods. Tired of the constant barking, jumping, or "selective hearing"? We make life with your dog enjoyable again.

Reliable Pet Obedience

Behavior Modification

Fixing complex issues like reactivity, anxiety, and household manners with clear, results-driven methods.

Puppy Foundations

Start your new best friend off on the right foot with proper socialization and engagement.

Breed Education

Our Signature Difference

Cooperative Husbandry

Because of our medical background, we help train for the "scary" stuff every dog needs to live a healthy life. We specialize in husbandry training, including:

  • Stress-free nail trims
  • Calmness during vaccines and vet exams
  • Cooperative grooming and handling
  • Medical handling preparation
For Working Dogs

Elite Protection & Sport

Precision. Protection. Performance.

For handlers looking for the ultimate level of safety and performance. Our expertise extends across a wide range of disciplines, ensuring every dog reaches their peak. Whether you are looking for a pre-trained dog or want to evaluate your dog for protection work, we have you covered.

Personal Protection Dogs (PPD)

Stable, reliable protectors. Development from the ground up or evaluation of your existing dog.

Sport Excellence

Professional coaching and a decoy on staff for APPDA, PSA, French Ring, and IGP.

Decoy & Trainer Services

Available for clubs and handlers preparing for trial or building working dog foundations.

High-Level Obedience

Competition-ready precision and off-leash reliability for the most demanding environments.
