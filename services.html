How You Train With Us

Training Programs.

Two primary paths. The one you choose depends on your schedule, your dog's temperament, and how fast you want results.

Option 01

Private Sessions

Hands-on coaching built around you and your dog. You train alongside us, so you leave every session with the skills to keep the momentum going at home.

Ideal for

Foundational work, behavior modification, sport handling, skill polishing.

Option 02

Board & Train

Immersive development at our facility. Your dog stays with us for a structured program that accelerates results, followed by transfer sessions so you can maintain the work at home.

Ideal for

Accelerated results, working prospects, complex behavior cases.

Additional Services

Medical Boarding

Expert care for dogs and cats, including those with medical needs. Our Vet Tech background means your pet is in hands that know how to handle complex cases.
